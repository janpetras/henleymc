Values and Principles

Henley was founded on three core values, Boldness, Innovation and Excellence. Over the years these have evolved and developed into a firm set of guiding principles which ensure that we maintain the highest standards of quality and customer service.

Our Guiding Principles:

Generating Ideas

We are clear that quality underlines everything that we do and we aim to deliver excellence as a minimum standard to our clients.

Strength and Pragmatism

We are proud to deliver a practical unique approach to learning founded on knowledge, strength and a highly diverse and practical consultancy team.

Respect and Tolerance

We respect the ideas, opinions and beliefs of others and above all we value the cultural diversity of our global client base.

Boldness and Innovation

We seek to challenge, change and improve all that we do and encourage those who attend our courses to do the same.
